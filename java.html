Sunny Weather Sunny weather occurs whenever there is no precipitation and few clouds present to cover the sun. Sunlight provides the energy that plants need to undergo photosynthesis, the process by which they make their own food.

Rain, Storms and Hurricanes Rain, which is also known as precipitation, forms in the clouds when water vapor, a gas, condensates and turns into large droplets of water.

Snow and Blizzards A snowstorm is a weather event characterized by wintery precipitation such as snow, sleet, or freezing rain. Snowstorms typically occur whenever temperatures are below freezing.
